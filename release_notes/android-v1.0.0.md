# Wreadom Android First Release Notes

Version: 1.0.0+1
Date: 2026-04-24
Platform: Android

## Play Store "What's new"

Welcome to Wreadom on Android.

- Read books with a focused, comfortable reader.
- Discover stories, authors, and community recommendations.
- Review books with text or audio.
- Comment, reply, like, and join reader discussions.
- Write, publish, and share your own work.

## Release Summary

This is the first Android release of Wreadom, a reading and writing community for discovering books, following authors, publishing original work, and discussing stories with other readers.

## Highlights

- Focused book reader with chapter navigation and reader controls.
- Book discovery through recommendations, authors, and community activity.
- User profiles, follows, notifications, and feed interactions.
- Reviews, comments, replies, likes, and discussion tools.
- Text-to-speech support for listening while reading.
- Audio review recording and playback.
- Writing and publishing tools for original books.
